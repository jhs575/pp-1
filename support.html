Buyer FAQ

Why was a hold/charge placed on my credit card?

When you place a bid on Cars & Bids, we put a hold on your credit card for the 4.5% buyer's fee based on your bid (minimum of $225, maximum of $4,500). This is a pending charge which is removed at auction end if you do not win the car, and within a few days will disappear from your card's transaction history. On the other hand, should you win, you will be charged at auction end.

Seller FAQ

Will you accept my car?

We're focused on cool cars, trucks, and SUVs from the modern era - which we're defining as the 1980s through the present day. If you're looking to sell a cool car from the 1980s, 1990s, 2000s, 2010s, or 2020s, we might be interested in listing it.

To find out if we'd accept your car, simply submit your car. It's free to submit and only takes a few minutes. We'll get back to you within 1-2 business days.
